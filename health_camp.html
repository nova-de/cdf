Mega Wellness (Health Checkup) Camp

The Chronic Disease Foundation (CDF) recently hosted a successful Mega Wellness (Health Checkup) Camp at Don Bosco Technical Institute (DBTI) in New Delhi. Under the expert guidance of Dr. Charu Dutt Arora, Head Consultant at AmeriHealth Home Care, and powered by the Asian Institute of Medical Sciences, Faridabad, the event was a significant step towards promoting health and wellness in the community. We are deeply grateful to Jai Shankar Sharma for his crucial support, which helped make this event possible. The camp aimed to provide comprehensive health services to both students and parents, encouraging a proactive approach to health management. Attendees benefited from a variety of services that addressed key aspects of their health and well-being.

Services Offered at the Camp:

  • Vitals Check & Anthropometry: Attendees had their vital signs, such as blood pressure and heart rate, monitored. Anthropometric measurements, including height, weight, and body mass index (BMI), were also recorded to help assess overall health status.
  • Consultation with a General Physician: A team of experienced general physicians provided medical consultations, addressing various health concerns and offering professional medical advice tailored to individual needs.
  • Assessment by a Nutritionist: Understanding the pivotal role of nutrition in maintaining health, the camp featured consultations with nutritionists. Participants received personalized dietary recommendations to enhance their nutritional intake and overall well-being.
  • Physiotherapy Sessions: To address physical health and mobility issues, the camp included physiotherapy sessions. Skilled physiotherapists conducted assessments and provided therapeutic exercises to help alleviate pain and improve physical function.
  • Fitness Session: Emphasizing the importance of physical activity, the camp offered fitness sessions. These sessions aimed to encourage regular exercise, enhance physical fitness, and educate participants on effective workout routines.
  • Mental Health Examination: Recognizing the significance of mental health, the camp provided mental health examinations. Qualified professionals conducted evaluations and offered guidance to promote mental well-being and address any psychological concerns.
The success of this event was a collaborative effort, thanks to the dedicated team at Don Bosco Technical Institute, whose hospitality and support were invaluable. The enthusiastic participation from the community highlighted the importance of such initiatives in promoting a healthy society.
At CDF, we are committed to organizing similar events in the future to raise awareness and provide accessible health services to all. Thank you to everyone who participated and helped make this Mega Wellness Camp a success.
For more information about our future events and how you can get involved, please visit our website or contact us directly. Together, we can make a significant impact on health and wellness in our communities.
