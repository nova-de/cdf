About CDF

MISSION & OBJECTIVES

SUPPORT

CDF offers a range of support services designed to address the physical emotional and social challenges faced by individuals with chronic diseases These services include counseling support groups, and access to resources that can help improve their quality of life

AWARENESS

The foundation plays a crucial role in raising public awareness about the impact of chronic diseases on individuals and society as a whole. By shedding light on the prevalence, symptoms, and consequences of various chronic conditions, CDF aims to reduce stigma and encourage early detection and intervention

EDUCATION

CDF believes that knowledge is empowering. The foundation provides educational programs and resources to help individuals better understand their condition, treatment options, and self-management strategies. This knowledge equips them to make informed decisions about their health and well-being

Empowering health through community and care, one wellness camp at a time.

PROGRAM & SERVICES

PATIENT SAFETY GROUP

Regular support group meetings provide a safe space for individuals to share their experiences, challenges, and successes while connecting with others who understand their journey.

EDUCATION WORKSHOPS

CDF hosts workshops on disease management, healthy lifestyle choices, and coping strategies to empower Individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to manage their conditions effectively.

PUBLIC AWARENESS CAMP

The foundation conducts public awareness campaigns through various media channels to increase understanding and empathy for those living with chronic diseases.

RESOURCES CENTER

An online and physical resource center offers a wealth of information, including pamphlets, books, and online resources that individuals and their families can access for further education and advocacy
